Sedan

The traditional three box car, with a bonnet, room for five or six(till bucket seats in the front row became the norm) people and a spacious boot. In the Indian car market, there is a sub category within sedans called compact sedans, which are a thing because of the favourable tax laws for cars under 4 metres in length. These cars also provide substantially greater boot space than hatchbacks of similar class/size. Sedans also exist in the category of executive class cars, that are usually larger, more luxuriously appointed and of higher price.

Suv

The very concept of a sport utility vehicle was born as rugged 4 wheel drive vehicles for use during World War II. The term “Sport Utility Vehicle” was coined during the 1980s and up until then they were called 4 wheel drive vehicles and / or trucks and jeeps. Some of the earliest such SUVs were made by Jeep, Willys and Land Rover. Later on, during the 1980s and later on, SUVs caught up in popularity.

MVP

se Vehicles are 5,6,7 or even up to 12 passenger vehicles that can also be used to haul stuff once you fold down the seats. They have become very popular since the first proper non-van MPV, the Chrysler Minivan debuted in the 1980s. Prior to this, anyone wanting such a vehicle had the choice of buying a Station Wagon or an actual Van. The success of the MPV has almost killed off the Station Wagon.

Hatchback

Any car that has an upward opening rear door(hatch) and has seating capacity of a maximum of five people. The very first hatchback design implemented in a car was the 1938 Citroen Traction Avant, which had a split tailgate, with an upward opening hatch and a downward opening gate. The next model year, made post WWII, had a full hatchback tail. The idea of a hatchback car grew in popularity throughout the 1960s and 70s and today is a huge chunk of the passenger car market, especially so in developing countries

Luxury

Luxury has been traditionally associated with limousines and sedans with extended wheelbases till the advent of luxury SUVs and sporty luxury cars. The currently relevant ideas of luxury involve sporty credentials and performance along with the availability of a large number of electronic gadgets with superb ride quality. The luxury car segment can be said to be the most accommodating segment, as it has possibly changed the most, by introducing and including cars into it's fold, as per public perception. Nowadays, a potential buyer can have a luxury car that could be a part of any class.
